ATS Resume Checker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

The short version

  • Your original CV file (PDF/DOCX) never leaves your device. It is read locally in your browser.
  • Only when you explicitly consent and click Analyze CV, the extracted text of your CV and non-identifying document-structure signals are sent over HTTPS to our backend and to OpenAI to produce your report.
  • You use the product through an anonymous account — no name, email, or registration.
  • You can delete your local data, any single report, or your entire anonymous cloud account at any time from the extension's Settings.
  • We run no analytics, no ads, no trackers, and no cookies, and we never sell data.

What is processed, and where

Locally in your browser (always): file validation, text extraction from your PDF/DOCX, document-structure analysis (page count, columns, tables, headings, contact-detail detection), the instant Format Score, and the optional "Basic (local) analysis". None of this leaves your device.

Sent to our backend only for an AI analysis (after your one-time consent): the extracted CV text, the document-structure signals, the file name, and an optional target role you may type. Our backend runs on Supabase in the EU (Stockholm region). It forwards the text and signals to OpenAI's API to generate the structured assessment; we request that OpenAI does not store the responses (store: false). Numeric scores are calculated by our own deterministic rules, not by the AI.

Never sent anywhere: your original file, and anything at all when you use only the local checks.

What is stored

  • On your device: the extracted CV text and signals, your settings and consent choice, your recent reports, and your private improvement-checklist marks.
  • In your anonymous cloud account: your AI analysis reports and usage records (needed to enforce the free monthly allowance). Reports are protected by row-level security — each anonymous account can only ever access its own data.
  • Operational logs: our backend logs timing and error codes only. It never logs CV text, quotes, names, contact details, or IP-address-based profiles.

Retention and deletion

Data is kept until you delete it. In the extension's Settings you can, at any time:

  • delete all local browser data;
  • delete any single report (locally and from the cloud);
  • delete your entire anonymous cloud account and everything in it.

Uninstalling the extension removes all local data; cloud data can be removed beforehand via Settings.

Processors

  • Supabase (hosting, database, authentication) — EU, Stockholm region.
  • OpenAI (AI analysis of extracted CV text) — processed under OpenAI's API data-usage terms; response storage disabled by us.

Your rights & contact

Because accounts are anonymous, deletion and export act on the account inside your browser. For questions or requests, open an issue on our support page (linked from the extension's Settings and the site footer).

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above changes and the extension's listing will reference the new version.
